About GMS

Global Marketing Services is a reputable medical business having its corporate office in Rawalpindi, Pakistan . We have established ourselves as market leaders in a number of specialized fields, including biomedical research, life sciences, clinical diagnostics, industrial diagnostics, interventional techniques, medical devices, biologicals, and pharmaceuticals, We are a member of a multinational network of businesses known as viSole .

We have faithfully supported the health sector since our founding in 2000 and have significantly increased our product offering. Our dedication to providing high-quality solutions, first-rate customer service, and prompt product delivery has helped us establish a solid reputation in the industry. We are proud to be the first medical business in Pakistan to receive accreditation for both Good Distribution Practise for Devices (GDPMD) and ISO 9001:2015.

We have a significant presence in all of Pakistan's main cities, including Peshawar, Lahore, Karachi, Quetta, Hyderabad, and more, in addition to our headquarters in Rawalpindi. We are able to meet the various demands of researchers, doctors, patients, hospitals, labs, and blood banks across the nation thanks to our extensive presence.

With a wide range of diagnostic tools and a commitment to innovation and improvement in the medical sector, we want to have a substantial impact on Pakistan's healthcare sector and act as a reliable partner for institutions and professionals globally

OUR MISSION

GMS’s focus is to provide Pakistan's medical industry, with innovative, sustainable, and turn-key technologies that prioritize patient safety and customer care ; mainly in the area of in-vitro diagnostics. We strive to make these technologies easily accessible to ensure that our clients can benefit from the latest advancements in the medical sciences

Area Of Interest

Delivering innovation

GMS is a leader in the medical industry, providing innovative and sustainable solutions for over 20 years. It has a strong customer base in Pakistan's in-vitro diagnostic sector and has also ventured into pharmaceuticals and medical devices. GMS has also spearheaded the ground-breaking initiative of local manufacturing of IVD solutions.

    • Pharmaceuticals
    • Medical Devices
    • Local manufacturing of IVD
