Illumina Genomics Solutions:

Sequencing and microarray products by Illumina for diverse applications.

Illumina is the global leader in the manufacturing of Next Generation Sequencing systems used by life sciences and researchers to isolate and analyze genes. Illumina’s proprietary microarray (Bead Array) technology uses microscopic silica beads that can carry samples through the genotyping process. The tests allow medical researchers to determine what genetic combinations are associated with various diseases, enabling faster diagnosis, better drug targets, and individualized treatment.
