OUR BLOGS

We, at Global Marketing Services, Pakistan, are privileged to have participated in the 2nd National Symposium on Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR) held at the esteemed National Institutes of Health Pakistan (NIH) in Islamabad. Under the able supervision of Dr. Muhammad Suleman, CEO of NIH, the event was meticulously organized, bringing together experts, professionals, and organizations committed to addressing the critical issue of antimicrobial resistance. Representatives from the World Health Organization (WHO) shared invaluable insights into global efforts to combat antimicrobial resistance. Their contributions were particularly enlightening, emphasizing the need for a unified approach to tackle this pressing global health issue. Professionals from bioMérieux, a leading player in Invitro diagnostics, added another layer of expertise to the seminar. Their presence highlighted the importance of innovative diagnostic solutions in the ongoing fight against AMR. International global companies were well-represented, fostering an environment of collaboration and shared responsibility. Networking opportunities allowed participants to exchange ideas and best practices, further strengthening the collective resolve to combat antimicrobial resistance on a global scale. Furthermore, our presence at the seminar reflects our commitment to staying at the forefront of healthcare advancements and collaborating with the global community to address challenges like AMR
